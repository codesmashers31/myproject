Our Story

Founded in 2025, Loklin began as a small initiative to help Pondicherry residents find reliable local services during emergencies.

What started as a simple directory has now grown into a comprehensive platform connecting thousands of residents with hundreds of verified service providers.

"Our goal is to make essential services accessible to everyone in Pondicherry with just a few taps."
