Frequently Asked Questions

You can register as a service provider by visiting our registration page and filling out the application form. Our team will review your application and contact you within 2 business days.

We currently cover all major areas in Pondicherry including:

  • White Town
  • MG Road Area
  • Lawspet
  • Muthialpet
  • Reddiarpalayam
  • Kuruchikuppam
  • Ganapathy Chettikulam
  • Mudaliarpet
  • Thavalakuppam
  • And surrounding areas

We're continuously expanding our coverage. If your area isn't listed, please contact us and we'll prioritize adding it.

Loklin has a rigorous verification process for all service providers:

  • Verified Badge: Look for the blue verification badge on their profile
  • Document Verification: We verify ID proofs, professional certifications, and business licenses
  • Customer Reviews: Check ratings and read detailed customer feedback
  • Background Checks: We conduct basic background verification for certain categories

You can also request to see specific certifications before booking a service.

We offer multiple secure payment options:

  • Online Payments: Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.)
  • Wallet Payments: Paytm, MobiKwik, and other digital wallets
  • Cash Payments: Available for in-person services
  • Payment Plans: EMI options available for certain high-value services

All online transactions are secured with 128-bit SSL encryption for your safety.

Our customer support team is available:

  • Phone Support: 8AM - 8PM (Monday-Saturday), 10AM - 4PM (Sunday)
  • Email Support: 24/7 with response within 24 hours
  • Live Chat: 9AM - 9PM daily through our mobile app
  • Emergency Support: For medical services, available 24/7

During peak hours, wait times may be slightly longer. You can also check our comprehensive Help Center for instant answers.

Our 5-star rating system helps maintain service quality:

  • Rating Criteria: Professionalism, Quality, Punctuality, Value, Communication
  • Verified Reviews: Only customers who booked through Loklin can leave reviews
  • Review Moderation: We check for authenticity and remove inappropriate content
  • Response Policy: Service providers can respond to reviews publicly

We take ratings seriously and may remove providers with consistently poor ratings from our platform.
